A Little About Me:

I’m passionate about building meaningful, user focused software and I thrive in project driven environments where I can design, develop, and deliver real solutions. My interest in tech started early. Growing up, I spent a lot of time playing Minecraft and became deeply curious about how the game actually worked behind the scenes. That curiosity even led me to dream about working at Mojang one day. What started as a love for a game slowly turned into a desire to understand the systems and logic that power software.

What fascinates me most about technology is how flexible it is. As artificial intelligence continues to grow, I often think back to watching 2001 A Space Odyssey, which introduced me to AI during my first Intro to Engineering course in high school. That moment really stuck with me and helped confirm that engineering was the path I wanted to take.

Technology as a whole genuinely excites me, and I enjoy constantly learning about how it can be used in new and creative ways.
