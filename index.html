A Little About Me:

I never planned to end up at Basgiath War College. While my peers spent years preparing for the deadly trials of the Riders Quadrant, I was meant for the safety of the Scribe Quadrant—surrounded by books, not battle. But life has a way of throwing you into situations you never saw coming, and sometimes the path you resist becomes the one that transforms you entirely.

I may not look like your typical dragon rider candidate—I'm smaller than most, my joints betray me at the worst moments, and I'd rather solve problems with strategy than brute force. But I've learned that true strength isn't always about physical power. It's about adaptability, quick thinking, and the courage to face impossible odds when everything you care about is on the line.

What drives me isn't just survival, though that's certainly part of it. It's the fierce loyalty I have for the people I love and the growing realization that sometimes the most dangerous battles aren't fought with swords and dragon fire, but with the choices we make when no one is watching. I'm still figuring out who I'm meant to be, but I know I'll face whatever comes next with everything I have.
